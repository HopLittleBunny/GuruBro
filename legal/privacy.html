Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

GuruBro (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the GuruBro mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

When you sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address or phone number (used only for authentication)
  • A unique user ID generated by our authentication provider

b. User-Generated Content

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Chat messages or text inputs you type into the app
  • Optional photos uploaded for style, grooming, or profile analysis

This content is considered user-generated content and is processed only to provide the requested features.

c. AI-Generated Content

GuruBro uses artificial intelligence to generate responses, suggestions, and feedback. AI-generated outputs are not stored as personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide AI-powered analysis and guidance
  • Generate chat replies, profile suggestions, and style feedback
  • Maintain account authentication and app functionality
  • Improve app performance and reliability (aggregated, non-identifiable)

3. Photo Handling & Retention

If you upload photos:

  • Photos are used only for on-demand analysis
  • Photos are stored temporarily in secure storage
  • Photos are automatically deleted shortly after processing
  • Photos are never used for advertising or training user profiles

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including:

  • Secure authentication via trusted providers
  • Restricted access controls
  • Encrypted communication channels

No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Your data may be processed by trusted third-party services strictly for:

  • Authentication
  • Cloud storage
  • AI inference and moderation

These services are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

6. Advertising & Tracking

  • No data is used for advertising
  • No data is sold to third parties
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking

7. Children’s Privacy

GuruBro is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Your Choices

You may:

  • Choose whether to upload photos
  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Request account deletion by contacting support

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
